Burger

Description

A hamburger is a food consisting of fillings —usually a patty of ground meat, typically beef—placed inside a sliced bun or bread rol

Ingredients

  • Bun
  • Chicken Zinger
  • Mayo
  • Kechtup
  • Salad

Steps

  1. Toast the bun and some Mayo on Bun
  2. Put Zinger on bun add some Salad
  3. Add some Kechtup and Serve
